Taego

8x8

After Kangjae Ma, the infamous inmate of the Ho San Prison, fought off an entire cell of convicts, the security footage of the riot went viral. Earning it the name of "Gulag tapes", this video inspired Sergei to create the Battlegrounds. Taego features a variety of terrain, from reed fields to the heart of Ho San Prison.
